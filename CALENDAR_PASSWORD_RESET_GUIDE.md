## 📍 How to Access Calendar

### For Everyone:
1. **After login**, click **"Calendar"** in the main navbar
2. Navigate to the calendar view
3. View tasks by month or day

### For Parents - Advanced Calendar Management:
1. Click **"Manage"** dropdown in navbar
2. Choose one of:
   - **📅 Recurring Chores** - Create daily/weekly/monthly chores
   - **🗓️ Holidays/Breaks** - Set dates when no chores are due
   - **⚠️ Bad Deeds** - Track negative behaviors and point deductions
   - **✅ Approvals** - Review and approve tasks kids submitted

## 🔐 How to Reset Password

### Step 1: Go to Login Page
- Click **"Login"** (if logged out)
- Or navigate directly to the login page

### Step 2: Click "Forgot Password?"
- On the login page, you'll see a new link: **🔑 Forgot Password?**
- Click it

### Step 3: Enter Your Username or Email
- Enter your username OR email address
- Click "Send Reset Link"

### Step 4: Check Your Email
- Look for email from ChoreTracker
- Click the reset link in the email
- The link is valid for 24 hours

### Step 5: Set New Password
- Enter new password (twice to confirm)
- Click "Reset Password"
- You're done! Login with new password
